Project Berkeley

A Citizen-led Coalition for Animal Rights Legislation, Business, and Culture in Berkeley, California

Animal Rights in Berkeley

Berkeley has historically been a hub for social movements and one of the most progressive cities in the US history. Project Berkeley is working to make Berkeley a world pioneer for animal rights.

Legislative Proposals

Project Berkeley works with the Berkeley City Council (BCC) and other local and national groups to pass important resolutions and legislation in Berkeley. Our sole goal is to promote animal rights and the protection of animals from the viewpoint of animals.

Our partners have included the Berkeley Animal Care Commission, Berkeley Progressive Alliance, the Sierra Club, the Nonhuman Rights Project, the Animal Legal Defense Fund, and various animal shelters.

Vegan and Animal-Friendly Businesses

Project Berkeley works to organize animal-friendly and vegan businesses in Berkeley. Additionally, Project Berkeley helps assist vegans and animal rights activists looking to make Berkeley their home.
