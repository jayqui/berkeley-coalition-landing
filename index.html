Project Berkeley

A Citizen-led Coalition for Animal Rights Legislation, Businesses, and Culture in Berkeley

Animal Rights in Berkeley

Berkeley is on track to become the most progressive city in the world with regard to animal rights, which is the developing social justice issue after women's rights, civil rights, disability rights, gay rights, and so on.

Legislative Proposals

Project Berkeley works with the Berkeley City Council (BCC) and other local groups like the Animal Care Commission, shelters, Sierra Club, Berkeley Progressive Alliance, and so on to pass important resolutions and legislation in Berkeley. Our sole goal is to promote animal rights and the protection of animals from the viewpoint of animals.

Vegan and Animal-Friendly Businesses

Project Berkeley focuses on bringing more activists and vegans to live and work in Berkeley, and building up the business, restaurant, and other instiutional support to normalize veganism and animal rights activism as the baseline.
